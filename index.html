Defend the Village

Village Defender is the current project that I am working on. It is the prototype of a future 3D melee-based story-driven game, centered in a fantasy world. Within this prototype, you will see the basis of the combat system and the state machines used to manage the animations and functions of both your player as well as enemies. This project's current state involves fighting off an enemy wave, in which, after defeating all enemies, a new wave with one more enemy will spawn and fully heal the player. Further updates are coming at a later time.
